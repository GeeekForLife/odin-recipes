What you Will Need

  • 1.5 lb. ground beef
  • .75 lb. sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 large onion -chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves -minced
  • 1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (15 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6 oz.) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 teaspoons salt -divided
  • 3 teaspoons Italian seasoning -divided
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves -divided
  • 2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup minced fresh flat-leaf parsley -divided
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese -divided
  • 15 oz. (1 ¾ cups) whole milk Ricotta cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese -divided
  • 2 large egg
  • 14 lasagna noodles

Things to remember

How to Layer!

  1. - Make a layer of uncooked lasgna noodles
  2. - Spread an even layer of ricotta cheese
  3. - Spread an even layer of homemade or canned meat sauce
  4. - Now repeat those layers TWO more times
  5. - FINALLY top it with a final layer of noodles and a generous amount of sauce and mozzerella and parmesan cheese

How to Bake Lasagna

  1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil
  2. Bake at 350 degrees F for 45 minutes
  3. Remove the foil and bake for 15 more minutes
  4. Let the lasagna rest for at least 15 minutes before slicing

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Make the meat sauce. Add beef and sausage to a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until meat is browned, about 6-8 minutes. Drain the fat from the meat. Return the meat to the skillet.
  3. Add the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 4 teaspoons salt, Italian seasoning, basil, and the black pepper. Stir until well combined. Cover and reduce the heat to low. Simmer the sauce for 30 minutes. Stir in 3 tablespoons of the minced parsley.
  4. As the sauce cooks, make the ricotta mixture. In a large bowl, add 1 cup mozzarella, the ricotta, 3/4 cup parmesan, 2 tablespoons of the parsley, the egg, 3 teaspoon salt, 3 teaspoon Italian seasoning, and basil. Stir until well incorporated.
  5. Cook the pasta al dente, according to it’s package directions.
  6. Assemble the lasagna. In a 13x9 inch baking dish, add a very thin layer of meat sauce (about ¼ cup). Layer 3 lasagna noodles, ⅓ of the ricotta mixture, and 1 ½ (level) cups of meat sauce. Repeat the layers twice.
  7. Add a final layer of the remaining noodles, meat sauce, 1 cup mozzarella, and ¼ cup parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil. Bake 15 more minutes.
  9. Let the lasagna cool for 15-20 minutes before cutting.
